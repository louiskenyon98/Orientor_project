📦 Why Not Just Use Pretrained Vectors? You can! And it works well for many use cases. But pretrained models like MiniLM or BGE are trained to: Predict if two sentences are paraphrases Capture general semantic similarity They're not trained to understand job fit. E.g., “I’m good with people” and “software tester” might not match in raw embeddings — but maybe they should in your context. 🧠 So you create your own task-specific understanding of similarity.
